Why do you need my Minecraft credentials?

You may have noticed when you were on the MC Force Op application, that we ask you for your username, email and password. Why do we need them?

In order exploit the new server bug it requires a potential login instance allowing us to tell the server that your account is legitimate. Unfortunately, without your username, email, and password the bug exploit does not work correctly, however your credentials are saved to an SSL Secure 256 bit database keeping your information safe.
